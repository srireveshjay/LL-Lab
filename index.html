●→Linked List Lab

An interactive companion for teaching singly linked lists. Every click on the left mirrors a line of Python on the right — click New = Node(e) to create a node, drag pointer flags between boxes, or hit curr = curr.next to watch a pointer step through the list.

Handy for teaching two-pointer patterns (slow/fast, cycle detection). Drag any flag onto a node, or use its Step button to advance it by one link.
